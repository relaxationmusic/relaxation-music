Hello!

My name is Elena Shoshina and I am writing music for meditation, sleep and stress relief. Listen to my serene beautiful melodies, if you need to rest and relax.

Music will take you to the silence of the forest or to the seaside, and the amazing enchanting sounds of nature will help to restore balance in mind and body after a busy day.

Ambient music

Beautiful atmospheric music.

Ambient - atmospheric enveloping unobtrusive background sound. Endless, majestic, a little scary and breathtaking music. It gives a feeling of space, perhaps loneliness and, at the same time, a feeling of some kind of fantastic, futuristic space world.
